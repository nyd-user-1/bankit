How to play

bank points, dodge the duds, quit while you're ahead
1Pick a category. You'll see 20 answers — but only 10 are really on the hidden list.
2Tap the answers you think made the list. Each correct tap is +1.
3Tap a wrong one and the round is over — you lose what you hadn't banked.
4Not sure? Hit Bank it & stop to lock in your points and walk away safe.
The whole game is knowing when to stop. Greed busts runs.
